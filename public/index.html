      What is this?

      Tubestack is a tiny reader that turns any YouTube video into a quiet, scrollable page. Drop a link in the box, and we'll fetch the captions, group them into paragraphs, and lay everything out in the kind of typography you'd expect from a long read — not an autoplay feed.

      Every paragraph is timestamped, so you can jump back into the video at the exact moment a quote landed.
